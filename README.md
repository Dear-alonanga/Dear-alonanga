🧭 About Me
I'm a GIS analyst and urban planner based in Kano, Nigeria, working at the intersection of geospatial technology, environmental management, and sustainable development. My work spans multi-country land use/land cover classification, ocean floor bathymetry, socioeconomic mapping, and community-level OSM contributions.
I'm also building a neem oil extraction startup — cold-pressed neem oil and neem cake for farmers, soap makers, and livestock keepers across Nigeria.
Currently open to: GIS consulting, freelance spatial analysis, research collaborations, and partnerships with NGOs working on agriculture, climate, or urban development in West Africa.
